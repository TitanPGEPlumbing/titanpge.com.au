Putting your trust in Titan P.G.E

Fully Licensed and Insured Plumbers

With years of experience under our belts, Titan P.G.E's reputation is built around customer trust and faith that the plumbing technician that arrives at your door is fully licensed and insured. Our plumbing team are regularly upskilled so you can remain confident that you receive the best service from the right plumbers.

Australian Standards Guarantee

All our work on your home or business is completed to follow the Australian Plumbing Standards AS/NZS 3500 and 5601. Paired with a 12 month warranty on all plumbing work, parts, and materials supplied and installed by us.

Free Quotes*

Here at Titan P.G.E we believe that getting information to you should be free, so we have taken steps to provide you with free over the phone, messenger, and email quoting for work on your home or business.*

*On-site consultations and home visits may incur a flat rate cost based on time of consult/visit or locations outside of standard servicing areas.

No Call Out Fee**

Here at Titan P.G.E we are don't believe that you should need to pay for a tradesman to sit in traffic on the way to your business or house for general plumbing services. This is why we have chosen to absorb the call out fees that other companies would normally charge.**

**Call outs after normal Australian hours of business (8am - 6pm), public holidays (including Sunday in South Australia), emergency services, and/or locations outside of standard servicing areas may incur a flat rate cost. In these situations, the costs will be advised up front and charged at the time of attendance.
